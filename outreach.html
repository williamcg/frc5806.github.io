FRC 5806 Outreach

Our efforts to connect with our local and engineering community.

Homecoming

Each year, in conjuction with our school's FTC team, the Basement Lions, #4236, we run a booth at our school's homecoming. It is a great opportunity for us to reach talk to alumnus, as well as allow kids to play around with our robots. It is a great opportunity for us to reach out to our local community. We love running the booth each year, as it allows us to introduce kids to robotics.

Barnes & Noble

In the fall of 2015, we ran a booth at a local Barnes & Noble bookstore, allowing kids and adults alike to interact with several ranger bots of ours and to learn about FIRST programs.
