About Us

The Basement Lions Robotics Team was founded in 2010 as an FTC team that consisted of only 6 people. Over the years the team has expanded to over 80 members, won countless awards, and broadened our impact in our community. This year we are splitting into FRC and FTC teams, and look forward to competing this year in FRC Stronghold.
